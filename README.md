üîç Dashboard Pages & Insights
1. Executive Summary
A high-level overview of the company‚Äôs performance, showing revenue trends and geographical distribution of delivery delays.

2. Logistics & SLA Analysis
Finding: The primary bottleneck is the Carrier Transit Time (9.28 days) rather than the Seller Preparation Time (2.71 days).

Insight: 75% of delivery time is spent with the carrier, directly impacting customer satisfaction.

3. Financial & Payment Analysis
Finding: 78.34% of payments are made via Credit Card.

Insight: Negotiating better processing fees with payment gateways could significantly increase net profit.

4. Customer Loyalty (NPS)
Classification: Customers are categorized into Promoters (9-10), Passives (7-8), and Detractors (0-6).

Insight: There is a clear negative correlation between delivery delay and review scores. Reducing delays is the fastest way to increase NPS.

5. Content & Sales Correlation
Finding: Products with more photos and longer descriptions tend to have higher sales and better ratings.

Insight: High-selling categories with low photo counts represent "Content Gaps" that can be easily optimized for more revenue.

üöÄ Strategic Recommendations
Carrier Optimization: Renegotiate contracts with carriers in high-delay regions to reduce the 9.28-day transit average.

Realistic SLAs: Adjust the "Estimated Delivery Date" provided to customers to match actual performance, reducing frustration.

Freight Cost Management: Reprice products in categories where the Freight-to-Price Ratio is too high (e.g., small, heavy items).

Service Recovery: Deploy a dedicated team to respond to Detractors (1-6 stars) within 24 hours to mitigate negative brand sentiment.

Content Enrichment: Launch a "Photos First" program for top-selling categories to ensure every product has at least 3-5 high-quality images.
